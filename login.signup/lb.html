Batangas State University

Lobo Campus

The Lobo Campus of Batangas State University, situated in Lobo, Batangas, offers quality education in agricultural, technical, and vocational fields. It focuses on developing professionals for economic growth and sustainable development. The campus offers state-of-the-art facilities, a hands-on learning approach, and a serene environment, making it an ideal place for academic and personal growth.
