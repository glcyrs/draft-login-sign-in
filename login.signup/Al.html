Alangilan Campus

Batangas State University – Alangilan Campus is the university’s premier hub for engineering, architecture, and technology. Located in Batangas City, it houses cutting-edge laboratories, innovation centers, and state-of-the-art facilities that foster excellence in science, technology, and research. Recognized as a national leader in engineering education, the campus is committed to producing globally competitive professionals and advancing sustainable development through innovation and academic excellence.
