About

Batangas State University

Batangas State University (BatStateU) is a leading institution of higher learning in the Philippines, committed to providing quality education and fostering research and innovation. With a diverse range of academic programs across its campuses, BatStateU is dedicated to producing graduates who are equipped to meet the demands of a rapidly evolving global landscape.

University Mission
A university committed to producing leaders by providing a 21st century learning environment through innovations in education, multidisciplinary research, and community and industry partnerships in order to nurture the spirit of nationhood, propel the national economy, and engage the world for sustainable development.

University Vision
A premier national university that develops leaders in the global knowledge economy.
