Batangas State University

Mabini Campus

Batangas State University's Mabini Campus offers higher education opportunities in Mabini, Batangas, focusing on technology and engineering. The campus offers programs under the College of Informatics and Computing Sciences, including General Engineering, aiming to produce globally competitive professionals. The Mabini Supreme Student Council supports student engagement and growth.
