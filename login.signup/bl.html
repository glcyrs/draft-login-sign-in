Batangas State University

Balayan Campus

The Balayan Campus of Batangas State University (BatStateU) offers a diverse range of academic programs, focusing on quality education, research, and community engagement. Located in the historically rich town of Balayan, Batangas, the campus provides modern facilities and a vibrant environment that fosters academic excellence and social responsibility. Students benefit from a unique learning experience that combines both rural and urban lifestyles, preparing them to contribute to local and global communities.
